Description & Commentary:

A small accessory carabiner, likely produced by Kong/Bonaiti for Black Diamond. Marking gives a use load limit of 150 Kg, pretty respectible and suitable for many odd climbing tasks.

This item can be found in the 1990 BD catalog as well as another BD catalog from an unknown year. It cannot be found in the 1995 and 1996 catalogs, and by 1997 Black Diamond was selling the plastic accessory Jivewire carabiner.
