Description & Commentary:

At the time of release, the lightest HMS style carabiner from a reputable manufacturer.

These have been part of my primary tree climbing kit for over a decade, and used for my closed hitch + minding pulleys when climbing on a moving rope system (MRS), usually DREC (Double Rope-End Climbing) on 9mm rope.

This version requires an additional turn to unlock than the v1 Vaporlock.
