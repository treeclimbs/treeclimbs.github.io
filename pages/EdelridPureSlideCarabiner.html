Description:

Since around 2010, locking carabiners have seen a huge increase in use across all vertical disciplines as designs become smaller and ligher. With this, auto-locking carabiners have diversified somewhat - with some users looking for high-security multi-lock autolocking carabiners and others looking for something more secure than a non-locking carbiner but faster to operate than a traditional screwlock carabiner. The Edelrid Pure Slide falls into this low-security category.

The locking slider is too exposed for my taste, and could easily open when contacting a foreign object or other piece of gear.

Other climbers report this locking mechanism fails, keeping the carabiner from closing fully. The HMS lockers tend to break more often - the locking mechanism will break fully, self-ejecting from the gate.
