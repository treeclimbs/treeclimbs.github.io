Summary:hollow - constructed from tubing not solid aluminum rod
Description & Commentary:

Body of the carabiner is contructed from a hollow tube for weight savings. Salewa probably produced this for Simond, as the body design & strength ratings (using kiloponds) almost identical to the Salewa Hollow Karabiner. The hollow tube seam is clearly visible on the ends of the nose and hinge.

Blue gate has large relieved sections, characteristic of Simond at this time.
