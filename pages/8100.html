Description & Commentary:

Not a carabiner, but a common arborist tool. The large hook will hold the handle of many chainsaws, or easily catch a loop of webbing or cord on the chainsaw lanyard.

A hole below the hook is large enough for a carabiner, or direct attachment of a chainsaw lanyard.
