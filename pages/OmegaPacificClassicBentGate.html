Omega Pacific
Classic Bent Gate

Brand:Omega Pacific
Item:Classic Bent Gate
Category:non-locking carabiners
Shape:asymmetric D
Material:aluminum
Nose:hook & pin
Profile:trapezoid
Rivets:flat spun
Strength Major:24 kN
Strength Minor:8 kN
Strength Open:7 kN
Features:flatspun rivets may have issues
Description:

A basic bent gate asym'd for use in quick draws. Omega Pacific produced budget carabiners, and this was the budget models of the budget brand. The Bent gate makes for a larger gate opening and easier clipping of the rope when used on a quickdraw.

Like other OP carabiners, the Classic uses flat-spun rivets, which are almost flush with the gate. They have sharp edges which catch and pull on the fibers of soft goods - very annoying.

On a personal note, I purchased a set of 5 (or maybe 6) new Omega Pac Classics quick draws around 2003 to flesh out my rock climbing rack for longer routes.

Upon arrival, I noticed 2 carabiners had loose rivets which had moved out of position. How these made it through QC and into the hands of a customer, I have no idea. I did get mine replaced (and upgraded to the much nicer JCs), but never saw a recall for the batch. I acquired this set of Classics to remind me of this issue.
