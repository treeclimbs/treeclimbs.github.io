Description & Commentary:

A solid workhorse professional carabiner, with ANSI rated gate, I-beam design, keylock nose.

These carabiners are often produced as whitelabel products for various companies including SafetyOne, Sterling, etc.

This model has a 120 deg twist gate, whereas most of the A333PS Kwicklock carabiners use a 90 deg twist gate.
