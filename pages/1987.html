Summary:3rd major revision of iconic HMS
Description & Commentary:

The third major revision of the popular Petzl Attache HMS carabiner. This model uses I-beam (H-beam) profile, but ditches the webbed (thick and thin) portions of the earlier Attache 3D.

Like the previous versions, this model retains the "Red-is-Dead" locking status indicator, debris clearning hole and aggressive sleeve knurling.

In 2024, the Petzl returned the full round profile to the Attache's basket primarily for belay use and introduced the Rocha as a lightweight large basket utility carabiner.
