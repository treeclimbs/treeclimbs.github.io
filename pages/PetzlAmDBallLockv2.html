Description:

The metal sleeved Petzl Ball Lock suffered from some early issues and was recalled in 2006. It eventually replaced the plastic sleeved version, and is my preferred auto-locking carabiner mechanism for tree climbing if I do not need a steel carabiner or ANSI gate-face rated carabiner. The only downside is that the Ball Lock is only available on the relatively heavy Am'D and William carabiners.
