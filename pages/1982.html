Description & Commentary:

Iconic carabiner with everything you need and nothing you don't. Smooth-running rope thanks to the round stock, an indelible red-is-dead unlocked indicator, snag-free keylock nose and beautiful symmetry.

For a time, Petzl sold the Attache alongside the hot forged I-beam Attache 3D. Eventually, Petzl discontinued the older models - leading some desperate Attache devotees to approach strangers at climbing sites about purchasing or trading for an under-appreciated first generation model made with full 12mm round stock.

Arguably, the only area for complaint is aggressive knurling which quickly abrades painted hardware and may hasten wear on soft goods. (And sure, the weight, but who brings dozens of Attaches on a climb?)

This example has an angled nose, rather than the straight nose found on another model with 23/7/6 kN ratings. The collection contains another angled nose model with different strength ratings (22/8/6 kN).
