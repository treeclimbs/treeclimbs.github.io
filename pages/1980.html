Description:

Iconic carabiner with everything you need and nothing you don't. Smooth-running rope thanks to the round stock, an indelible red-is-dead unlocked indicator, snag-free keylock nose and beautiful symmetry.

With the release of the hotforged v2 Attache, some Attache devotees have been known to approach strangers at climbing sites about purchasing or trading for an under-appreciated v1 model.

Arguably, the only area for complaint is aggressive knurling which quickly abrades painted hardwear and may hasten wear on soft goods. (And sure, the weight, but who brings dozens of Attaches on a climb?)
