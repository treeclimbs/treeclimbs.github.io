Description & Commentary:

The 3rd generation of Petzl Ball Lock mechanism, and the 2nd gen of the metal sleeve. It operates easily with only a button press and 45 degree throw.

Compared to the first generation of metal sleeve ball lock carabiners, the button of this model protrudes slightly more, but is still wider than the original plastic sleeve version. I find it equally easy to operate as the previous metal-sleeve version.

The opposite side of the sleeve has two vertical texture bars which make the sleeve easiser to rotate, which could be a problem for the previous version in poor weather, wearing gloves or with sweaty hands. The rest of the knurling is also more aggressive. Overall, these are welcome improvements in usability.

The ball-lock mechanism is my preferred auto-locking mechanism for tree climbing if I do not need a steel carabiner or ANSI gate-face rated carabiner. The only downside is that Petzl only offers the Ball Lock on relatively heavy Am'D and William carabiners.
