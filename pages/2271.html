Description & Commentary:

Lightweight screwlock carabiner from the early '90s. Gate action is snappy and the slightly bowed spine fits nicely in the hand.

Wild Country touted the "bowback" design in a 1992 ad in Climbing Magazine, claiming a wider gate opening and light weight. The same ad also highlighted the "quick gate action" as another safety feature to avoid gate open loads (presumably due to gate flutter/shutter).

The pictured carabiner in this ad has a 2400 kg rating (vs 2300 kg on this example).
