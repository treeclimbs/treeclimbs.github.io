Description & Commentary:

The autolocking version of the Petzl Spinball operates easily with only a button press and 45 degree throw (vs the Spinball's 120 deg throw). The plastic sleeve had finger grooves with a molded fingerprint texture. The button protrudes significantly more than the metal sleeved version and I find it more difficult to depress fully.
