Description & Commentary:

Extra large steel carabiners designed for use with scaffolds, ladders and the like are common in the fire/rescue and industrial access. The DMM Scaffolder is made for similar purposes, but bent from much lighter aluminum. In the hand, this huge carabiner feels very light for what it is, although it lacks the keylock nose and ANSI rated gate of the DMM Ultima.
