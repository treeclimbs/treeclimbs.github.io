Description & Commentary:

A simple clawlock carabiner with no signs of rivets - these could be hidden by corrosion or the gate is attached some other method.

This example is unmarked, but I aquired these as part of a group of vintage carabiners including a 123 KB10-S locking carabiners which is very similar to this model.

I am unfamiliar with the 123 brand of carabiners, but it appears they have been producing carabiners since 1967. If anyone has vintage catalogs, please contact me.
