Description & Commentary:

Tanglefree, a waterfowl hunting company, sells this large accessory carabiner to store decoys or other gear, with a large basket for holding lots of equipment and the large size makes it easier to operate with gloves on.

The finish is rough, the gate resists opening at about the point shown in the photo above, the gate ends do not extend beyon the carabiner body, which could result in the end pulling out of the carabiner. Also, for an unrated carabiner by a company called "Tanglefree", the nose hook seems unnecessary...
