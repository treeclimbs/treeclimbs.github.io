Description & Commentary:

Latchless, with a noticably square nose. The latchpin is square (some other models use a round pin), BREVETE SGDG is smaller than on ID#2082. Pyramidal proof test punch mark present to the right of the strength rating, indicating post 1965 manufacture.

See ID#2083 or ID#2080 for more info on latchless Pierre Allain carabiners and history of proof testing.
