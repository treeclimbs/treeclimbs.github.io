Description & Commentary:

Hugh Banner's late 90's captive quickdraw carabiner. A captive pin was added to the HB HiSport model to encourage loading along the major axis of the carabiner. Pictured on the HB website from 1997 to 2001, and appear to be offered for sale through the end of Hugh Banner (2005). This example appears to be the same as the 1997-2001 model, but weighs in at 60 grams rather than the 78 grams listed on the HB website.

An earlier version from 1992 used a captive eye (slot) much like the DMM Mamba. This example with the captive pin was probably easier to produce since it could use the same tooling as the HiSport model.
