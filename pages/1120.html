A standard full-sized carabiner with a solid gate. This sports a "dual-trac" gate - a unique channelized front gate which makes it feel a bit like a wiregate for clipping purposes.

Dual-trac style gates are not found on modern solid-gate carabiners, which is why I've marked this one with a ★.
