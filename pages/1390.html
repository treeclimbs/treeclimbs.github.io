Description:

C.A.M.P. used a double bend for the nose-end of the gate, whereas most wiregate carabiners have a single bend at the nose. This presents a wide frontal surface, which makes clipping rope one handed extremely easy. Unfortunately, the wiregate protrudes significantly beyond the sides of the carabiner, making it highly likely to be opened if dragged along a rock surface.
