Description & Commentary:

A simple mid-sized carabiner for general climbing / quickdraw use. Similar to the HiLite found on the 1997 website, but without the forged markings. Similar to Mountain Technology quickdraws.

The T-beam construction on the corners with a central flat looks very "Omega Pacific like" but perhaps is DMM manufacture given rivet & batch number style and high polish finish? Unsure of the relationship or if Hugh Banner produced these themselves.

The spine's central flat forms small indentations on the exterior of the spine, which make the carabiner comfortable to grip and clip. Smooth gate action, with well-tuned let-off of the spring pressure when the gate is fully opened. Overall, a very nice carabiner for an era of notch & pin gates & T-beam profiles.
