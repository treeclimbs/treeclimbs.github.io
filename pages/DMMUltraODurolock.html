Description:

Unique twist-lift-twist-open quadruple-action. The locking mechanism has a strong spring and is extremely secure.

Locking oval carabiners are very popular with tree climbers for use with a closed friction hitch or mechanical friction hitch (such as the Akimbo or Rope Runner). They are often attached or detached while the ground before or after a climb. The extra security in this application outweighs the inconvenience of a quadruple-action carabiner.
