Description & Commentary:

Lightweight 1990's T-beam carabiner design, with very similar designs sold by Stubai, Kong and Mammut.

The T-beam is formed in part by long flat sections on the carabiner spine, basket and runner end, which also provide a clean surface for the markings along the spine. The exterior of the carabiner body adjacent to these flats have distinctive raised ridge visible when viewing the carabiner on edge.
